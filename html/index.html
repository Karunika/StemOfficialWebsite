    Welcome to STEM

    About the server
    A server dedicated to both tutoring and technical discussion in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics) topics for students, professionals, and enthusiasts of all ages (13+) and backgrounds. Whether you're a student or just interested in learning about any of these subjects or related fields, this server is for you! Feel free to start/join in a discussion in any of our subject-related channels or just chill in any of our general ones.

    ❯ Getting Started
    Head on over to #get-roles to assign yourself some roles.
    ✪ If you're here to ask STEM questions, read #how-to-ask.
    ✪ Check out the pins 📌 in each channel for subject-related resources!

    ❯ Expectations
    ✪ Respect all Discord ToS, Guidelines, and server staff.
    ✪ Post in the appropriate channel.
    ✪ Use English.
    ✪ Respect your fellow STEM members. Be civil and don't be toxic.
    ✪ Do not spam.
    ✪ Do not post inappropriate content.
    ✪ No unsolicited advertising or any kind of promotions within the server or DMs.

    You can report misconduct using #🔕contact-mods.
